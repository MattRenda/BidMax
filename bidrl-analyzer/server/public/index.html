How it works

From listing to max bid
in seconds

BidMax runs quietly in the background while you browse. No extra clicks, no separate tools — just numbers on every card.

Open any BidRL auction page. BidMax detects all the lots automatically and queues them for analysis the moment the page loads.

2

AI prices each lot

Every lot title is analyzed against real, local Facebook Marketplace prices — not retail, not eBay. Conservative estimates to protect your margin.
