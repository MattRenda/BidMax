# BidMax Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2, 2026**

> This is a template starting point, not legal advice. Have a qualified attorney
> review it, and make sure it matches your actual data practices and the data
> disclosures you submit to the App Store and Google Play.

This Privacy Policy explains how BidMax LLC ("BidMax," "we," "us")
collects, uses, and shares information when you use the BidMax app and website
(the "Service").

## Information we collect
**You provide / via Google sign‑in:**
- Account info from Google: your **email address, name, and Google account ID**.

**Automatically:**
- A **device identifier** we generate to enforce free daily usage limits.
- **Usage and analytics data**: actions in the app (e.g., scans, analyses
  revealed, upgrades), app version, device type, and OS.
- **App settings** you choose (ROI target, buyer's premium, thresholds, theme),
  and which lots you've revealed or requested — stored **on your device** and, in
  some cases, associated with your account on our servers.

**Purchases:**
- Subscription status and transaction identifiers from **Apple, Google, Stripe,
  and RevenueCat**. We do **not** receive or store your full payment card
  details; those are handled by the payment provider.

We do **not** intentionally collect precise location or sensitive categories of
personal data.

## How we use information
- Provide and operate the Service (sign‑in, analyses, Top Picks, subscriptions).
- Enforce free‑tier usage limits (via the device identifier and your account).
- Process and validate subscriptions and entitlements.
- Improve and secure the Service, debug issues, and understand usage trends.
- Communicate with you about your account or the Service.

## How analyses work
To estimate resale value, we send **lot details (such as title, description, and
image URL)** to our AI provider, **Anthropic** (Claude), for analysis. These are
third‑party auction listings, not your personal data.

## How we share information
We share information only with service providers that help us run BidMax, under
agreements limiting their use of it:
- **Google** — authentication (sign‑in).
- **Apple / Google Play / Stripe / RevenueCat** — subscription billing and
  entitlement management.
- **Hosting / database providers** (e.g., Railway, Supabase) — to run our
  servers and store account data.
- **Anthropic** (Claude) — to generate resale estimates from listing data.
- **Analytics providers** — to measure product usage.

We may also disclose information to comply with law, enforce our Terms, or
protect rights and safety. We do **not sell** your personal information. If we
are involved in a merger or acquisition, information may transfer as part of that
transaction.

## Data retention
We keep account information while your account is active and as needed for the
purposes above or to meet legal obligations. You can request deletion (below).

## Your choices and rights
- **Access / deletion:** request a copy or deletion of your account data by
  emailing **MatthewJrenda@gmail.com**. You may also delete your account in the app /
  on the website [if/when available].
- **Subscriptions:** manage or cancel in your App Store / Google Play settings or
  your website account.
- Depending on where you live (e.g., California, EEA/UK), you may have additional
  rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, or to object to certain
  processing. Contact us to exercise them.

## Children
BidMax is intended for adults (18+) and is not directed to children. We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the minimum age
in your jurisdiction).

## Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information.
No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot
guarantee absolute security.

## Third‑party links and services
The Service references third‑party sites (such as auction listings on BidRL).
Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this one.
